About

I am an Economics student at Cornell University with interests in macroeconomics, global markets, and investment research.

Through this journal, I analyze how economic data, geopolitical developments, central bank policy, and market expectations influence asset prices across rates, commodities, equities, and broader financial markets.

My goal is to develop a deeper understanding of how investors interpret information, price risk, and react to changing economic conditions. Topics covered include inflation, monetary policy, interest rates, commodities, equity markets, and major global macroeconomic developments.
